The Magic of Kleinwalsertal Gentian Schnapps

Nestled in the picturesque landscape of the Kleinwalsertal, surrounded by impressive mountain peaks and mystical forests, Christl and Harald Riezler have perfected their passion for traditional macerates. In the heart of this secluded high mountain valley, known for its pure air and untouched nature, their gentian schnapps is created, a true symbol of living regionality and alpine culture.

The product

A taste experience that is worth it

If you thought the mountain air in Kleinwalsertal was fresh, wait until you try our gentian schnapps! This schnapps is not just a drink, but an alpine expedition in liquid form. It starts with a tart, fresh breeze that boldly ventures to the tops of the highest Alpine peaks. In the mouth, it then unfolds its full potential: strong, with an earthy note so deep that even the marmots could be jealous.
But the real kick is yet to come! Like a hike through untouched fresh snow in winter, the schnapps leaves a trail of spicy nuances that dance on the tongue long after the last sip. A bit like having taken on a mountain spirit - exciting, a little mystical and absolutely unforgettable.
Whether enjoyed neat or as a bold accompaniment in a cocktail, our gentian schnapps from Riezler is more than just a drink; it is a tribute to the robust, wild heart of the Kleinwalsertal. Cheers to the mountains!

producer

Christl and Harald Riezler: Keepers of Alpine Distilling Art

The Riezler couple are devoted to the art of distilling schnapps. In their small maceration workshop in Riezlern, where tradition and modernity come together harmoniously, they maintain methods that have been handed down over generations. Their deep understanding of the regional ingredients and the alpine climate enables them to create a schnapps of exceptional quality.
Kleinwalsertal, often described as "the most beautiful dead end in the world", offers ideal conditions for the production of premium spirits thanks to its isolated location and pure natural springs. The region is known not only for its scenic beauty, but also for its rich history and culture, which gives the gentian schnapps an additional dimension of authenticity and depth.
Enjoy a strong sip of tradition from Kleinwalsertal and let yourself be seduced by the purity and intense taste of our gentian schnapps.

Questions & Answers

Despite its origins in the occasionally blue gentian blossom (don't worry, we use the yellow variety), our schnapps remains as clear as a mountaineer's conscience. The real magic of gentian schnapps lies not in its color, but in its strong, earthy taste that brings the mountain air into your living room!

Our gentian schnapps is as versatile as a Swiss Army knife! Although it is a real highlight on its own, it can also be used fantastically in the kitchen. A shot in a strong beef broth or to deglaze a mountain lamb roast - your taste buds will go mountain climbing!

Historically, gentian root has been valued for its digestive properties. While we don't offer any medical advice, let's just say that a glass of our gentian schnapps will definitely lift your spirits! And who knows, maybe your stomach will be happy too.
